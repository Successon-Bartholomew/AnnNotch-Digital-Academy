This course covers all aspects of digital marketing including SEO, social media, content marketing, email marketing, and analytics. You will learn how to create effective digital marketing strategies to grow businesses online.

  • Create and manage social media marketing campaigns

  • Analyze marketing data to improve campaign performance
  • Manage paid advertising campaigns(PPC)
