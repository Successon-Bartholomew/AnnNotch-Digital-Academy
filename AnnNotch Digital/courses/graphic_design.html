Graphic Design Masterclass

Master the principles of graphic design and create stunning visuals using industry-standard software.

This masterclass wil introduce you to the fundamental principles of graphic design and teach you how to use Adobe Creative Suite to create professional designs. You will learn color theory, typography, layout design, and brading concepts.

What You Will Learn

  • Master the principles of visual design and composition
  • Create professional designs using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
  • Develop skills in typography, color theory, and layout design
  • Build a professional portfolio of design projects
  • Create logos, branding materials, and marketing collateral
  • Design for both print and digital media
